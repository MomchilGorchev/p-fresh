About

Hi there!
My name is Momchil, I am a Bulgarian web developer living currently in London, UK. Im seriously interested in JavaScript development for the past two years.

I am working for a technology company and some of the interesting things that I do are developing JavaScript solutions to consume JSON API's and to extend platform's functionality; building single-page applications from pixel-perfect PSD files, using vanilla JavaScript or frameworks like MeteorJS and AngularJS, to support the multi-platform products of the company.
I also try to involve cutting-edge technologies into any suitable project, either internal or external.

Always aiming to write quality, abstracted and reusable code, I get inspired by other people who share the same passion. I am big fan of Meteor JS, Open-Source and all sorts of web animations.

In the meantime I am changing nappies and do other stuff.
